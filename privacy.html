Privacy Policy

Petal Hop · last updated June 13, 2026

Petal Hop collects no data. The app does not connect to the internet, contains no analytics or tracking, shows no ads, and has no accounts or logins. Nothing about you or your device is gathered, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

What is stored on your device

The only thing Petal Hop saves is your in-game progress — which levels you've cleared, which abilities you've unlocked, which dewdrops you've found, and your sound preference. This is kept locally in the app's sandboxed storage on your own Mac and never leaves it. Deleting the app removes it.

No third parties

Petal Hop uses no third-party services, software development kits, or advertising networks. There are no analytics providers and no crash-reporting services.

Children

Petal Hop is suitable for all ages and, because it collects no data, complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K by design.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
